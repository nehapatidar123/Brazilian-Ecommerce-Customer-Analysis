# 📊 Brazilian E-Commerce Customer Analysis (Power BI + SQL)

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project analyzes the **Brazilian E-commerce (Olist) dataset** to understand customer distribution, order trends, and retention patterns.

The objective was to combine **SQL-based analysis with Power BI visualization** to convert raw transactional data into **business insights and strategic recommendations**.

---

## 📁 Dataset

* Source: Brazilian E-commerce Public Dataset (Olist)
* Includes:

  * Customer demographics
  * Order transactions
  * Purchase timestamps

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Analyze customer distribution across states and cities
* Identify geographic concentration and dependency risks
* Understand order trends over time
* Evaluate customer retention behavior (new vs repeat)
* Build an interactive dashboard for decision-making

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)
* SQL (Data Analysis & Querying)
* DAX (Measures & KPIs)

## 📌 Key Metrics

* Total Customers: ~96K
* Total Orders: ~99K
* Orders per Customer: ~1.03
* Repeat Contribution: ~6%

---

## 💡 Key Insights

### 🔹 Customer Distribution

* ~42% of customers are from São Paulo (SP)
* Top 3 states (SP, RJ, MG) contribute ~66% of total customers
* Customer base is **highly concentrated geographically**

---

### 🔹 Urban Dominance

* Major cities like São Paulo, Rio de Janeiro, and Belo Horizonte dominate
* Customer presence is **urban-centric**
* Smaller cities remain underpenetrated → growth opportunity

---

### 🔹 Customer Behavior

* Orders are only slightly higher than total customers
* ~93% customers are **one-time buyers**
* Indicates **very low retention and repeat purchasing behavior**

---

### 🔹 Business Risk

* Heavy dependency on a few states (especially SP)
* Lack of geographic diversification increases risk
